⚡ 1-click usage logging

Make one button per account. Click it on your Claude usage page and your tracker updates itself — no screenshot, no typing.
⬆︎ drag me
On a computer:
  1. Drag the purple button up to your bookmarks bar.
  2. It saves as a bookmark. Rename it if you like (e.g. “Log ”).
  3. Open your Claude usage page, then click that bookmark. Done.
On a phone (copy the code instead)
Add any page to bookmarks, edit it, and paste this as the address. Then open it while on the usage page. (Fiddly on phones — the screenshot method is easier there.)
Repeat for each account by changing the nickname above.
Nothing you type here is saved to the internet — the button just carries the nickname you enter.
